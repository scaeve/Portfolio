About me

I am a motion graphic designer and upcoming developer based in The Netherlands. This year I started following Open-ICT in Utrecht. At Open-ICT I learn to bloom as a starting developer and how to properly work with team cases. Before Open-ICT I spent a lot of time in the Adobe Ecosystem, especially After Effects, Premiere Pro and Photoshop. Besides using photo and video-editing tools I also made fun little Python projects in my free time. It was through these experiences that I really wanted to follow Open-ICT. The freedom Open-ICT gives you, really wants to make you think out of the box. I’m most experienced in Adobe After Effects. I’ve had multiple editing accounts on Instagram which allowed me to express my thoughts and creativity through short little video’s, and my viewers seemed to love them. I got loads of direct messages asking me if I could make personalised video’s for them. I made commissions from 2018 - 2021.
